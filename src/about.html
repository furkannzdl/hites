About Us

As Hites, we were founded in 2013. With a rich engineering team consisting of Bioengineer, Biomedical, Mechatronics, Electrical-Electronics engineers, we provide R&D services in the fields of energy, medical, and artificial intelligence. We have the ability to design in mechanical, electronics, and software fields.

Hites is progressing towards becoming a strong R&D company. We aim to continuously enhance lives with the most advanced technology together with our team members. Human health, sustainability, innovation, reliability, and quality are our priorities.

Our Vision

To be a reliable and high-quality R&D company that constantly improves people's lives with advanced technologies that can be used by people, focusing on sustainability and innovation.

Our Mission

By producing advanced solutions through our interdisciplinary engineering knowledge in the fields of energy, medical and artificial intelligence, and through mechanical, electronic and software design.
